Tourist attraction areas

Kandy

Home to the Temple of the Tooth, a UNESCO World Heritage site and a cultural hub of Sri Lanka.

Galle

A coastal city with a rich colonial history, home to the iconic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Mirissa

A serene beach town known for its whale-watching tours, surf spots, and stunning sunsets.

Ella

A lush hill town famous for its tea plantations, scenic hiking trails, and the iconic Nine Arches Bridge.

Nuwara Eliya

A picturesque town known as 'Little England,' surrounded by tea plantations, waterfalls, and colonial-era architecture.

Yala National Park

Sri Lanka's most popular wildlife sanctuary, offering safari experiences with sightings of leopards, elephants, and diverse wildlife.

Sigiriya

An ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring breathtaking views and stunning frescoes.

Anuradapura

An ancient city with a rich Buddhist history, home to centuries-old stupas and sacred relics.

Polonnaruwa

The well-preserved ruins of Sri Lanka's medieval capital, showcasing impressive temples and statues.

Trincomalee

A coastal paradise with pristine beaches, a natural harbor, and a rich cultural and religious heritage.

Information

Sri Lanka

Overview: Sri Lanka is an island nation known for its vibrant culture, deep-rooted history, and stunning natural beauty. From ancient temples and bustling cities to lush tea plantations and palm-fringed beaches, it offers a rich variety of experiences. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a cultural deep dive, Sri Lanka offers it all. Its diverse landscapes and rich history make it a must-visit destination, appealing to both nature lovers and history enthusiasts.

Best Time to Visit


  • Weather and Seasons: The best time to visit Sri Lanka depends on the region. For the southwest coast and Hill Country (Colombo, Galle, Ella), the best period is from December to March. For the east coast (Trincomalee, Arugam Bay), the best time is from April to September.

  • Off-season Travel: If you prefer fewer crowds and more affordable travel, the monsoon seasons (May to August for the southwest, October to January for the northeast) offer a quieter experience with lush green landscapes.

Cultural Highlights


  • Festivals and Traditions: Sri Lanka celebrates a variety of cultural festivals, like Vesak (celebrating the Buddha’s birth), Sinhala and Tamil New Year, and the Esala Perahera in Kandy, which showcases the island’s Buddhist heritage.

  • Cultural Sites: Explore ancient cities like Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, or visit revered temples like the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, where a sacred relic of the Buddha is housed.

Travel Tips


  • Language: Sinhala and Tamil are widely spoken. Some useful phrases include “Ayubowan” (Hello in Sinhala) and “Vanakkam” (Hello in Tamil).

  • Currency: The local currency is the Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). It’s advisable to exchange money at authorized exchanges or banks.

  • Transport: Sri Lanka has a well-connected network of buses, tuk-tuks, and trains. For more convenience, you can hire a private driver.

Local Cuisine


  • Popular Dishes: Sri Lanka is famous for its flavorful Rice and Curry, the street food favorite Kottu Roti, and spicy coconut-based sides like Pol Sambol.

  • Where to Eat: Street food stalls are a must-try for an authentic taste of local dishes. Popular spots include Galle Face Green in Colombo and bustling markets in major cities

Safety and Health


  • General Safety: Sri Lanka is generally a safe destination. Always be mindful of personal belongings, and stay hydrated, especially in hot weather.

  • Health Tips: Make sure you're vaccinated for common travel diseases and protect yourself from mosquito bites. Carry sunscreen and stay hydrated throughout your trip.

Visa and Entry Requirements


  • Visa Information: Most travelers need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA), which can be applied for online before arrival.

  • COVID-19 Regulations: Ensure you check the latest COVID-19 restrictions, vaccination requirements, or other necessary travel documents before visiting.
